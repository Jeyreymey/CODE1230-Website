Data Set General Information

The Data Set

The data set is a care or assistance of a child/children provided to another person to assist with daily activities, due to disability, long term illness or problems with old age, within two weeks prior to the census.

Why Its collected

The reason this question is asked, was to capture all types of unpaid childcare within Australia. This includes care for Grand Children, and children of relative, friends and neighbours.
